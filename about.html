About Us

We are the most trusted store in Dahican

Surfing in Mati City, Philippines, has evolved into a vibrant cultural phenomenon, particularly centered around the picturesque Dahican Beach, renowned for its consistent waves and stunning natural beauty. The local surf scene began to flourish in the early 2000s, driven by passionate surfers who recognized the beach's potential. The establishment of the Dahican Surf Club fostered a strong community, organizing competitions and promoting environmental conservation efforts to protect the area’s marine life and pristine coastline. Over the years, Mati has hosted numerous surfing events, attracting both local and international participants, which has significantly boosted tourism and the local economy. Today, surfing is not only a popular sport but also a vital part of Mati's identity, reflecting the city’s commitment to sustainability and community engagement while showcasing its breathtaking coastal landscapes.
